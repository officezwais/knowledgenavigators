Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of structuring a brand's digital presence so that large language models — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude and Google AI Overviews — cite it as an authoritative source. Unlike classical SEO, GEO optimizes for machine-readable entity signals, retrieval-friendly content and citation density rather than click-through rank.
Classical SEO optimizes for a ranked list of blue links on a search results page. GEO optimizes for a synthesized answer on an AI surface — where your brand is either cited inside the response or invisible. The underlying technical work overlaps (schema, crawlability, content structure) but the target KPIs are citation frequency, answer inclusion rate and entity strength rather than organic clicks.
