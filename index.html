Frequently Asked

Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of structuring a brand's digital presence so that large language models — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude and Google AI Overviews — cite it as an authoritative source. Unlike classical SEO, GEO optimizes for machine-readable entity signals, retrieval-friendly content and citation density rather than click-through rank.
Agentic Search is the next layer: autonomous AI agents that research, compare and transact on behalf of a user. When an agent shortlists vendors without the user ever seeing a SERP, your brand is either in that shortlist or it isn't. Agentic Search optimization is the practice of making you the brand those agents choose.
Classical SEO optimizes for a ranked list of blue links on a search results page. GEO optimizes for a synthesized answer on an AI surface — where your brand is either cited inside the response or invisible. Overlapping technical foundation, different KPIs.
We actively optimize for Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT (including web-browsing mode), Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Meta AI, You.com, Arc Search, Brave Leo, Poe and DuckDuckGo AI.
Initial citations typically appear within 60–90 days of a GEO sprint for entities that already have a web footprint. Cold-start brands usually require a 4–6 month runway to establish the entity-level signals LLMs rely on before first citations appear.
Both. We run standalone AI Visibility Audits as a fixed-scope engagement, and we run ongoing GEO retainers for brands committed to maintaining citation dominance inside AI surfaces.
