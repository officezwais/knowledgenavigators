01 / The Berlin thesis

Berlin is where the next decade of European SaaS is being written — and none of it is optimized for AI search.

Berlin's tech ecosystem is dense and globally coupled in a way no other German city is. N26, Zalando, Delivery Hero, Personio, Contentful, HelloFresh, Pitch, Tier — companies selling to buyers who evaluate vendors in English, on mobile, inside an LLM. By the time a procurement lead at an enterprise in London or São Paulo reaches your website, the shortlist is already written.

And it's written by AI. A Gartner-style comparison used to be a PDF. Now it's a Perplexity query. The vendor named in that Perplexity answer gets the meeting. The one that isn't, doesn't.

Most Berlin growth teams are still running the 2021 SEO playbook — content farms, link audits, keyword clusters. The playbook works less every quarter. We rebuild it for the answer-engine era, in the language your team actually works in (usually English), shipped as engineering tickets your team can merge.
