Note on confidentiality

Why most of our case studies are private.

A large share of our work is for private banks, pharma companies, legal firms and enterprise B2B brands in regulated industries. These clients do not publish their agencies — not because the work is secret, but because naming an "AI search agency" publicly would invite competitive benchmarking their brand teams would rather avoid.

We respect that. Every engagement begins with a mutual NDA. Reference calls can be arranged with prospective clients under NDA — real conversations with real operators, not curated marketing quotes.

Anonymized engagements

Representative work.

CASE 01 / DACH SAAS

Category-defining B2B SaaS platform

Situation: Strong classical SEO. Zero presence in AI answer engines. Buyers were moving early-stage research to ChatGPT and Perplexity.

Work: Full GEO engagement. Entity consolidation. Schema rebuild. Citation-source strengthening.

Outcome: Cited in 60%+ of tracked buyer prompts within four months. Qualified pipeline attributable to AI referrals tracked separately from classical organic.

CASE 02 / CH PRIVATE BANKING

Swiss independent wealth manager

Situation: Prospective clients asking Claude and ChatGPT about the firm before any human contact. No visibility layer to monitor it.

Work: Full AI visibility audit. Rewrite of key "about" and investment philosophy pages for extraction. Wikipedia/Wikidata presence review.

Outcome: Moved from "brand not found" to cited in every tracked wealth-advisory prompt within eight weeks. Ongoing quarterly share-of-voice reporting.

CASE 03 / UK ENTERPRISE B2B

FCA-regulated fintech platform

Situation: Enterprise buyers using Copilot and ChatGPT to build RFP shortlists. Brand repeatedly missed cut.

Work: AEO rewrite of 40 priority pages. Schema unification. Third-party citation-source campaign. Technical crawl fixes.

Outcome: Named in Copilot shortlist prompts within 60 days. Measurable lift in enterprise-grade inbound RFPs.

CASE 04 / DE E-COMMERCE

Premium DTC brand (100+ SKU)

Situation: Perplexity and Google AI Overview were answering category questions using competitor content.

Work: Product schema rebuild. Category AEO architecture. TL;DR answer blocks on every category and product page.

Outcome: Cited in Google AI Overviews for 30+ category queries. Incremental revenue attributable to new answer-engine traffic.

CASE 05 / NL SAAS

European HQ of US-origin SaaS

Situation: Well-known in US, underrepresented in European AI answers. Content library was English-only, entity-ambiguous.

Work: Entity disambiguation. Wikidata registration. Per-market landing architecture. Full GEO methodology.

Outcome: Cited across all five priority EU markets in ChatGPT and Perplexity. Brand mention frequency tripled across tracked prompt panel.

CASE 06 / AT LIFE SCIENCES

Austrian medtech scale-up

Situation: Medical buyers researching devices in LLMs. Entity presence in biomedical knowledge graphs was effectively zero.

Work: Technical SEO foundation. Medical schema architecture. Wikidata and PubMed presence strategy. Citation-source campaign.

Outcome: Brand moved from invisible to cited in clinical-question prompts. HCP inbound measurably lifted.
