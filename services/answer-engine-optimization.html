01 / What AEO is

AEO is the craft of being quoted — cleanly, confidently, and with attribution.

Every answer engine works the same way underneath. It pulls candidate passages from the open web, ranks them by extractability and trust, and builds a response from the strongest few. If your paragraph cannot be cleanly lifted into a 40-word answer, it does not matter how good the underlying insight is. Another brand's paragraph will be.

AEO is the discipline of making sure yours is the paragraph the engine chooses. That means writing content the way answer engines parse it: short lead-in sentences, atomic facts, entity-stable phrasing, schema that confirms what the prose already says, and internal linking that signals topical authority without polluting the extraction target.

This is what classical SEO agencies cannot ship. They optimize for the click. We optimize for the citation — because the citation is now the click.

03 / Methodology

Our five-step AEO engagement.

Every engagement follows the same disciplined workflow. No black boxes. No "AI magic." Every decision is measurable and reversible.

  1. 01

    Extraction Audit

    We crawl your priority pages the way answer engines do, then score every paragraph on extractability: can a 40-word answer be cleanly lifted, does it read atomically, is it entity-resolved, does the surrounding context reinforce or dilute it. Output: a ranked list of passages to rewrite and passages to delete.

  2. 02

    Question-First Architecture

    We map the real prompts your buyers send to ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini — not keyword lists. Each priority question becomes a dedicated URL or an on-page H2 with a TL;DR answer block designed for extraction, followed by depth that satisfies the slower SEO crawl.

  3. 03

    Schema & Entity Reinforcement

    FAQPage, HowTo, Article, Product, Organization and Person schema — all with entity IDs that resolve to your canonical knowledge graph node. This is the layer where most agencies copy-paste FAQ schema blocks and stop. We treat schema as machine-readable proof that the prose is telling the truth.

  4. 04

    Citation Source Strengthening

    Answer engines pull from a trust graph, not the open web. We strengthen your presence in the specific sources they weight heavily: Wikipedia and Wikidata entities, industry directories, high-authority publications, structured datasets. Off-page AEO is where the durable compounding happens.

  5. 05

    Measurement & Iteration

    We track brand share-of-voice inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Google AI Overviews and Copilot across a rotating prompt panel of 200–500 queries per client. Monthly deltas. Per-engine breakdowns. No vanity metrics. Iterate on what moves, kill what doesn't.

06 / FAQ

Answer Engine Optimization — common questions.

What is Answer Engine Optimization?+

AEO is the practice of structuring content so AI-powered answer engines — Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot — can extract a clean, attributable answer from your pages and cite your brand as the source. Classical SEO optimizes for clicks. AEO optimizes for citations.

How is AEO different from GEO?+

GEO is the broader discipline of becoming a source LLMs rely on when they generate answers. AEO is the on-page execution layer: how you structure a paragraph, heading, schema block and entity so the engine lifts your answer and attributes it to you. We deliver them as one engagement in most cases.

Does AEO replace traditional SEO?+

No. Answer engines still rely on indexed, crawlable, fast-loading content with clean HTML and correct schema. AEO sits on top of a healthy technical SEO foundation. We audit both together — see our technical SEO service.

How long until we see AEO results?+

Citations in Perplexity and Google AI Overviews typically shift 30–60 days after changes are indexed. Training-cycle engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) move slower — expect meaningful share-of-voice changes at 90–180 days. We measure both fast and slow signals in parallel.

Do you work in German and English?+

Yes. All engagements are delivered in German or English. Multilingual entity architecture across DACH is a specialty — most agencies treat it as an afterthought and lose citation share as a result.
