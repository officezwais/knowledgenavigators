Answer
Engine
Optimization.

The on-page discipline of being quoted — cleanly, confidently, with attribution — by AI answer engines.

Read the Full Service → Read the Playbook

Definition. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring content so that AI-powered answer engines — including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude and Copilot — can extract a clean, attributable answer from a page and cite the source.

Etymology

The term gained traction as featured snippets and generative answer boxes began replacing classical search result pages. AEO describes the narrower execution layer of Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): specifically, the on-page work of making a passage quote-ready for an answer engine.

What AEO covers

AEO includes extractability audits, TL;DR answer blocks, FAQPage and HowTo schema, entity-stable phrasing, atomic factual sentences, and the structural choices that let an answer engine lift 30–60 words of your content with confidence.

AEO vs GEO

GEO is the broader strategic discipline. AEO is the specific craft of making individual passages citeable. Most engagements deliver both together.
